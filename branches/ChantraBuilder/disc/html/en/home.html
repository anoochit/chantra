Welcome!

OpenDisc is a collection of carefully selected, high quality open source software for Windows. You can browse the software through the menu above, where you'll find more information about each program.

OpenDisc is a completely volunteer project; everybody involved in development works on the project in their spare time, and this continuing development depends on donations from people like you. If OpenDisc has been useful for you, please consider a donation to support development. Either visit the website at www.TheOpenDisc.com, or with PayPal using donate@theopendisc.com.

If you'd rather help us with your spare time, see our website for how you can help!  We're always after suggestions for new software, and help preparing new programs for inclusion on the next version.
