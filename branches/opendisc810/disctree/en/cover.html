For people who like to give OpenDisc to friends, family, workmates and even distribute on a larger basis, we've created official artwork for you to print and use. Until recently artwork has been a community effort and sometimes versions went entirely without any artwork at all. This official artwork will now be available at the same time every new version of OpenDisc is released, so you can use it right away! Full colour artwork is unfortunately available for this release.

All images are available on this disc and can be found in /CoverArt/ when browsing the disc through My Computer. If you have trouble with the OpenDisc browser opening when you try to look at the disc through My Computer, right click on the disc itself (usually named D:\) and select Open.

The images are:

disc-label.png    A Disc Label for printing onto compatible inkjet-printable discs; label is 300dpi and in the PNG format.

cover-front.png    Card Sleeves protect the disc and give a polished, professional look to your OpenDisc. The cover is 300dpi and in the PNG format. We've gone with something different to the regular cut-out and glue card sleeves - our covers are simple origami. No mess, no fuss and no waste-paper at the end; all you need is an A4 sheet of paper.

cover-back.png    Once you've printed your artwork, put the artwork back into the printer upside-down and print this folding guide on the back of the sheet. Once that's done easy instructions are available to fold your cover. After you've folded your first card sleeve you'll be an expert!
